Updates

This page will be where we post any problem clarifications and other general updates. It will probably be a good idea to check here every couple days during competition.

Injection Readded November 29, 2014

Injection should be working again but may go down again - just let us know in IRC or on the feedback page if this happens.

General Updates November 29, 2014

The checker for Project Eratosthenes has been fixed. Fast Math's point value has been decreased to 120, and scores will be automatically recalculated to reflect this. Injection is down and has been removed from the problems page. We will add it back once it is fixed.

Problem Clarification: Flowchart November 29, 2014

The "flag" should be the output of the loops in the order they are shown (without line breaks or spaces), not the letter choices.

Scoreboard November 29, 2014

There was a problem with the scoreboard today, where you get points for incorrect flags. It should be fixed now; don't be alarmed if you lost points, we made a mistake in calculation.

One more day! November 28, 2014

If you didn't know, a few days ago we decided the game Singularity will not be available during competition due to lack of active game developers, but the problems can still be accessed on the problems page once the competition starts. With only one day left till our launch, we have put up a couple sample problems on the About page. It may also be worth looking at our Learn page to get a head start on learning if you need some help with Python or Linux! And if during competition, you find any bugs, please use the Feedback page to notify us or you can message us privately on the IRC. Abusing bugs may lead to disqualification or ineligibility for prizes - we want the competition to be as fair as possible.

Also, the end time for the competition has now been updated from 11am Dec 6th to 10pm Dec 6th to allow teams to have more work time (especially on the weekend since most competitors are students and probably more busy during the week).
