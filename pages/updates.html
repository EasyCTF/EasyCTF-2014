One more day! November 28, 2014

If you didn't know, a few days ago we decided the game Singularity will not be available during competition due to lack of active game developers, but the problems can still be accessed on the problems page once the competition starts. With only one day left till our launch, we have put up a couple sample problems on the About page. It may also be worth looking at our Learn page to get a head start on learning if you need some help with Python or Linux! And if during competition, you find any bugs, please use the Feedback page to notify us or you can message us privately on the IRC. Abusing bugs may lead to disqualification or ineligibility for prizes - we want the competition to be as fair as possible.
