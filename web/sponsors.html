Educoin

EduCoin (EDU) is an open source peer-to-peer digital currency. Just as Bitcoin is radically re-inventing the future of money, EduCoin will reshape the future of education everywhere. EduCoin aims to be the worldwide standard for transactions between students, educators, and third parties.

HSCS

HSCS.io empowers passionate computer science high school students to enrich computer science learning in their communities. We will do this by aiding student run events, educating students in CS, and enabling students to run their own events.
